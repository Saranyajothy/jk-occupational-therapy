JK Occupational Therapy Services is a private practice and owned by Jothikumar Ramakrishnan, Occupational Therapist with 18 years of experience in various fields of Occupational Therapy in three countries such as India, United Kingdom and Ireland.

Jothikumar’s Profile:

I’m a member of Association of Occupational Therapists in Ireland (AOTI) and All India Occupational Therapist’s Association (AIOTA). I’m a CORU registered Occupational Therapist. I gained my graduation in Occupational Therapy (OT) from the Tamil Nadu Dr M.G.R. Medical University in India in 2004 and I completed my Post Graduation in Posture, Seating and Wheelchair Mobility across the life course from University of Limerick in 2015. I’ve 18 years of experience including Hospital, Clinic, School, Therapy and community settings. During my career I have worked in three different countries such as India, United Kingdom and Ireland.

I’m a committee member and Treasurer for Housing Advisory Group, AOTI. I started my private practice in Occupational Therapy in 2016 and I have completed various assessments, interventions and reports for my clients all over Ireland. I’m specialised in

  • Seating and wheelchair assessments for clients at home and residential settings
  • Housing Assessment ranging from minor to complex home modifications
  • Functional assessments
  • Home Safety assessments
  • Cognitive assessments
  • Fitness to drive assessments (for elderly)
  • Moving and handling assessments for residential settings

I’ve provided services to Brothers of Charity, Rehabcare, Post-Polio Support Group, Western Care, University of Limerick and various Nursing homes & Residential service providers.

I’m also passionate about teaching Occupational Therapy and I’ve present guest lectures in Ireland and India. I’m a Guest Lecturer for Saveetha University, India.

If you need any Occupational Therapy services or wish to enquire about services provided, please feel free to contact us.
